Instruction

  1. Log into the one.com Control Panel.
  2. Click DNS settings on the Advanced settings tile.
  3. Go to DNS record
  4. Under create new record, click TXT.
  5. Enter the TXT string under Value.
  6. Optionally enter a TTL, or leave it empty to default to 3600 seconds.
